Privacy Policy

Toy Video AI — Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

Toy Video AI ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Photos: When you take or select a photo of a toy, the image is sent to our cloud servers solely for the purpose of generating an animated video. Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics through Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve our service. This includes app launch events, feature usage, and crash reports. This data does not personally identify you.

Purchase Data: In-app purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, or any other personal information. The app does not require account creation or login.

Camera and Photo Library

The app requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to capture or select photos of toys for video generation. These permissions are optional and can be managed in your device Settings.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google): For cloud functions (video generation processing) and anonymous analytics. Firebase's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

fal.ai: For AI video generation. Images sent for processing are used solely to generate the requested video and are not retained. Privacy policy: fal.ai/privacy

Children's Privacy

Toy Video AI is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal data to function.

Data Retention

Generated videos are stored temporarily on cloud servers for delivery to your device. Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months by Firebase. No personal data is retained.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is typically no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any concerns about your data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
