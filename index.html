How it works

Toy Video AI brings your toys to life using artificial intelligence. Take a photo of any toy, pick a magical scenario, and watch as your toy becomes an animated character in a short video.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Magic Dust?

Magic Dust is the in-app currency used to generate videos. Each video costs 25 Magic Dust. New users start with 100 Magic Dust (4 free videos). You can purchase more in the Magic Shop.

What are Stars?

Stars are earned each time you generate a video. You can spend Stars to unlock new scenarios like Space Adventure, Dance Party, and more.

Why did my video fail?

Video generation requires a stable internet connection. If generation fails, your Magic Dust will not be deducted. Please try again with a good connection.

How do I get a refund?

For refund requests on in-app purchases, please contact Apple Support through the App Store, or email us directly.
